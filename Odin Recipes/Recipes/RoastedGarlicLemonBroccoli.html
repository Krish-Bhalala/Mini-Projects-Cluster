Roasted Garlic Lemon Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 2 heads broccoli, separated into florets
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Toss broccoli florets with extra virgin olive oil, sea salt, pepper, and garlic in a large bowl.
  3. Spread the broccoli out in an even layer on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until florets are tender enough to pierce the stems with a fork, 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Remove and transfer to a serving platter.
  6. Squeeze lemon juice liberally over broccoli before serving for a refreshing, tangy
